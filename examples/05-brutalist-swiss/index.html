[02] MANIFESTO / 我们相信

TYPOGRAPHY
IS POLITICS.

Design is not decoration. It is the careful arrangement of intention. Every letter carries a weight, every line carries a voice.

We reject the noise of infinite choice. Three colors. Two families. One grid. Constraint is the soil of clarity.

The page is a surface, not a stage. We work in the quiet tension between full and empty — and let the reader finish the sentence.

A brand is not a logo. It is a promise, kept consistently, across every surface it touches. We build those surfaces.

[04] PROCESS / 方法论

FOUR STEPS.
NO BLACK BOX.

每一个项目都穿过这四个房间。没有惊喜，没有魔法 — 只有可被追踪的决策。

01

LISTEN / 聆听

在设计任何东西之前，我们先做研究。访谈、档案、实地观察 — 把模糊的问题翻译成具体的词语。

PHASE 012 — 4 WEEKS
02

BUILD / 构建

从一个概念出发，我们同时推进三条设计线索：系统、符号、声音。每周评审，每周收敛。

PHASE 024 — 8 WEEKS
03

TEST / 验证

把设计放回真实世界：打印、上线、朗读、在路上看、在手机上读、在会议室开投影看。

PHASE 032 WEEKS
04

SHIP / 交付

交付不仅是文件。我们带走走流程的每一份文档与规范 — 让品牌在你手里，而不是在我们手里。
